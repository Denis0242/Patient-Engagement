# Experiment Plan

## Experiment Question
Can targeted reminders improve D30 retention and treatment completion?

## Hypothesis
Patients receiving personalized follow-up reminders will have higher D30 retention and treatment completion than patients receiving the standard journey.

## Groups
- Control: standard follow-up process
- Variant: reminder + care navigation support

## Primary Metric
D30 retention

## Secondary Metrics
- Treatment completion rate
- Follow-up completion rate
- Outcome success rate

## Guardrail Metrics
- Cost per completed treatment
- Patient support workload
- Patient satisfaction

## Decision Rule
Ship the intervention if D30 retention and treatment completion improve without materially increasing cost per successful outcome.
