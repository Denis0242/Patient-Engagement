# KPI Definitions

| KPI | Definition |
|---|---|
| Total Patients | Count of unique patients |
| D1 Retention | Share of patients retained after 1 day |
| D7 Retention | Share of patients retained after 7 days |
| D30 Retention | Share of patients retained after 30 days |
| Engagement Rate | Share of patients with engagement score greater than or equal to 7 |
| Treatment Completion Rate | Share of patients who completed treatment |
| Follow-up Completion Rate | Share of patients who completed follow-up |
| Outcome Success Rate | Share of patients with successful outcomes |
| Treatment Cost | Total cost associated with treatment events |
