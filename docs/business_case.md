# Business Case

This project helps healthcare stakeholders monitor patient engagement and outcomes across the digital care journey.

## Problem
Patients may begin their healthcare journey through a portal or appointment flow but fail to complete treatment or follow-up.

## Opportunity
By monitoring retention, completion, cost, and patient risk segments, teams can identify where to intervene and improve patient outcomes.

## Target Users
- Healthcare operations leaders
- Digital health product teams
- Care navigation teams
- Executive stakeholders
- Analytics teams
